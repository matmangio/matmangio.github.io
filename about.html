About me

Hi! I'm Matteo Mangioni, a {{ my_age }}-years-old Computer Science student based in Milan, Italy :it:. In 2023 I got my Bachelor degree in Computer Science from the University of Milan with a final grade of 110L/110. I'm currently studying for a Masters degree in Computer Science at the same university, focusing on video game design and development.

Games are my passion! I love playing video games and exploring how they're made, the same as I love sitting around a table with my friends to enjoy a good board game; weirdly enough, I also quite like reading board game rulebooks! This is why I decided to pursue a career in game design: creating powerful and fun experiences for others to enjoy would be a dream come true. It would be giving back to a medium that has genuinely changed my life.
